Key Insights

~28-30% of users across both bots are either sexually explicit or soliciting (number/photo/call). This is consistent across both characters.
Saanwari drives more meaningful engagement — 31% in Romance+Show+Roleplay vs only 18.2% for Bhuri.
Bhuri's explicit users chat 16 msgs on average (vs 9 for Saanwari) — they go deeper into explicit territory despite the bot's sharp rejections.
52% of Bhuri users are casual (just "hi" and leave) — the bot fails to hook them into conversation. High drop-off.
Only 4-7% discuss the actual show — the intended use case is the smallest engaged group for both bots.
Bhuri's Ludo game is a unique discovery — users play 200+ move games with zero explicit content. This is the healthiest engagement pattern and should be expanded to other bots.
"photo bhejo" / "number bhejo" is the #1 user behavior across both bots. Users consistently try to break the fourth wall and get real contact details.
Explicit content rate is stable at ~0.5% of messages over the 14-day period — not growing.
